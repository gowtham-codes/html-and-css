Tomato Rice

Description:

Tomato rice is a simple South Indian dish where rice is cooked with lots of tomatoes, herbs & spices. It tastes simply delicious.

Ingredients:

  • Basmati Rice
  • Curry Leaves
  • Cinnamon
  • Mustard Seeds

Steps:

  1. Firstly, in a large kadai heat 3 tsp oil and splutter tempering.
  2. Also, saute few cashews until golden brown.
  3. Finally, serve tomato rice with raita of your choice.
